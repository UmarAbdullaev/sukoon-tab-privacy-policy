Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 13, 2026

Sukoon Tab (“we”, “the extension”) is a Chrome new tab extension. This policy explains what information is used, where it is stored, and which third-party services may be contacted when you use the extension.

Summary

  • We do not operate user accounts or sell personal data.
  • Most data stays on your device or in your Chrome sync profile.
  • Location is used only if you enable features that need it.
  • Network requests are limited to public content and weather APIs.

Data stored on your device

The extension stores the following locally using Chrome extension storage:

  • Settings (theme, widget toggles, clock format, prayer method, name, and similar preferences) in Chrome sync storage, so they can sync across signed-in Chrome profiles if you use Chrome Sync.
  • Daily content cache (today’s ayah, hadith, and dua) in local storage to reduce repeat network requests.
  • Saved favorites (ayahs, hadiths, and duas you choose to save with Sukoon Tab Plus) in local storage on your device.
  • Location coordinates (latitude and longitude only) in local storage when you allow location access for prayer times or weather.
  • Shortcuts you add on the new tab page, stored locally.

Geolocation

If you enable prayer times or weather, the extension may request your device location through the browser’s geolocation API. This is used only to calculate prayer times, show approximate local weather, and reverse-geocode a city label. Coordinates are stored locally so the extension does not need to request location on every new tab.

You can deny location access. Prayer times and weather will not work until location is available, or until you use the location update option in Settings after granting permission.

We do not receive or store your location on our own servers.

Third-party services

When you use Sukoon Tab, the extension may contact these services:

  • Al Quran Cloud — fetches Quran Arabic text and English translations for daily ayahs. Only ayah references are sent, not personal information.
  • Open-Meteo — fetches weather data when the weather widget is enabled (Sukoon Tab Plus). Requests include approximate coordinates.
  • BigDataCloud — reverse-geocodes coordinates to a city name for display. Requests include approximate coordinates.

Hadiths and duas shown by default are bundled inside the extension and do not require a network request.

Payments and licensing

Sukoon Tab Plus purchases are processed by Lemon Squeezy. When you activate a license key, the extension contacts Lemon Squeezy to register and validate your license. Your license key and activation instance ID are stored locally on your device.

Children’s privacy

Sukoon Tab is a general-purpose new tab extension and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top will reflect the latest revision.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the developer through the Chrome Web Store listing or the support contact listed there.
