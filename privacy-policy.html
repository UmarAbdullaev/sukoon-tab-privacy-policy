Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 16, 2026

Sukoon Tab (“we”, “the extension”) is a Chrome new tab extension. This policy describes what information the extension uses, where it is stored, and which third-party services may be contacted when you use Sukoon Tab.

Summary

  • We do not operate user accounts or sell personal data.
  • We do not run application servers that store your personal data.
  • Most data stays on your device or in your Chrome sync profile.
  • Location is used only when you enable prayer times or weather.
  • Sukoon Tab Plus purchases are handled by Dodo Payments, our payment and licensing provider.

Information we do not collect

Sukoon Tab does not:

  • Require you to create an account with us
  • Collect your name, email, or payment details directly
  • Track browsing history outside the new tab page
  • Send analytics or advertising data to the developer
  • Store your location on developer-operated servers

Data stored on your device

The extension stores the following using Chrome extension storage:

  • Settings — theme, widget toggles, clock format, prayer calculation method, display name, and similar preferences. These are saved in Chrome sync storage and may sync across devices if you use Chrome Sync.
  • Daily content cache — today’s ayah, hadith, and dua, stored locally to reduce repeat network requests.
  • Curated ayah catalog cache — a local copy of the ayah reference list used for daily ayah rotation, refreshed periodically from a developer-hosted static file when updates are available.
  • Saved favorites — ayahs, hadiths, and duas you save with Sukoon Tab Plus, stored locally on your device.
  • Location coordinates — latitude and longitude only, stored locally when you allow location access for prayer times or weather.
  • Shortcuts — links you add on the new tab page, stored locally.
  • Plus license data — if you activate Sukoon Tab Plus, your license key, a browser-specific activation ID, activation label, and last validation timestamp are stored locally. License data is not synced through Chrome Sync.

Geolocation

If you enable prayer times or weather, the extension may request your device location through the browser’s geolocation API. This is used only to calculate prayer times, show approximate local weather, and reverse-geocode a city label for display.

Coordinates are cached locally so the extension does not need to request location on every new tab. You can deny location access; prayer times and weather will not work until location is available, or until you update location again from Settings after granting permission.

We do not receive or store your location on our own servers.

Third-party services

When you use Sukoon Tab, the extension may contact these services:

  • Al Quran Cloud (alquran.cloud) — fetches Quran Arabic text and English translations for daily ayahs. Only ayah references are sent, not personal information.
  • Open-Meteo (open-meteo.com) — fetches weather data when the weather widget is enabled (Sukoon Tab Plus). Requests include approximate coordinates.
  • BigDataCloud (bigdatacloud.com) — reverse-geocodes coordinates to a city name for display. Requests include approximate coordinates.
  • Dodo Payments (dodopayments.com) — activates, validates, and deactivates Sukoon Tab Plus license keys when you choose to use Plus. See the next section.
  • GitHub Pages (pages.github.com) — may fetch an updated curated ayah reference list (Quran surah/ayah references only, no personal information). A bundled copy ships with the extension and is used if the remote file is unavailable.

Hadiths and duas are bundled inside the extension. The curated ayah list is also bundled, with optional periodic updates from the developer-hosted file above.

Each third-party service has its own privacy policy. We encourage you to review those policies if you want more detail about how those providers handle data.

Payments and licensing (Sukoon Tab Plus)

Sukoon Tab Plus is optional. Purchases are processed by Dodo Payments, which acts as the merchant of record. When you buy Plus, Dodo Payments collects payment and customer information (such as email) according to its own privacy policy. We do not receive your payment card details.

After purchase, Dodo Payments provides a license key by email. If you activate Plus in the extension:

  • The extension sends your license key and a browser activation label to Dodo Payments to register this installation.
  • The extension periodically sends your license key and activation ID to Dodo Payments to confirm the license is still valid.
  • If you remove the license from Settings, the extension asks Dodo Payments to deactivate this browser’s activation seat.

Checkout opens in a new browser tab on Dodo Payments’ website when you choose to upgrade. The extension does not embed payment forms inside the new tab page.

Dodo Payments privacy information: dodopayments.com/privacy

Chrome permissions

Sukoon Tab requests the following Chrome permissions:

  • storage — save settings, favorites, cache, shortcuts, and license data
  • alarms — refresh daily content and re-check Plus license status
  • geolocation — calculate prayer times and weather when enabled
  • tabs — open Dodo Payments checkout in a new tab when you upgrade to Plus
  • favicon — show website icons for shortcuts you add

Children’s privacy

Sukoon Tab is a general-purpose new tab extension and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top of this page reflects the latest revision. Material changes will be reflected in that date.
